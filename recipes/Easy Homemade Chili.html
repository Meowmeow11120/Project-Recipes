Easy Homemade Chili

Ingredient

  • Beef
    •

    The recipe starts with a pound of ground beef. You can use ground turkey if you prefer.

  • onion
    •

    Cooking a chopped onion with the ground meat adds tons of flavor.

  • Canned food
    •

    You'll need a can of tomato sauce, a can of stewed tomatoes, and a can of kidney beans.

  • spices
    •

    This homemade chili is spiced with chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
